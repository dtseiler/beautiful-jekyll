The former due to "Jessica" being in the playlist for Guitar Hero II, the latter because not only did it provide me a rhyme and reason (and workaround) for my Oracle listener hanging, but it is one of the most well-written and presented diagnoses that I've seen, even busting out tcpdump to sniff the wire.

I somehow missed Jeremy's post when he originally wrote it a month ago, and stumbled across it today via Doug Burns. Doug mentions that he first noticed a problem when he saw multiple listener processes. A-HEM:
