--- layout: post title: Data and Datatypes date: '2007-02-26T11:38:00.000-06:00' author: Don Seiler tags: - oracle - datatypes modified_time: '2010-11-19T22:48:47.492-06:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7032512792942232766.post-8316393861719453731 blogger_orig_url: http://www.seiler.us/2007/02/data-and-datatypes.html --- Somehow while reading through some Oracle blogs this weekend, I found myself re-reading a post from Tom Kyte last year about mis-used datatypes. Such things as people using string datatypes to hold date values, or people using VARCHAR2(4000) for every field in every table. Tom does a fantastic job (as always) explaining the many reasons this is just awful, including loss of data integrity and performance.

Always worth a (re)read.
